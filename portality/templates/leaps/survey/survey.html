Welcome to the LEAPS student survey

This survey contains six parts. You must complete every part in order to successfully save your responses. It should take no more than 30 minutes to complete, if you prepare in advance.

Please have details of your qualifications, work experience, and possible University or College applications to hand before you start this survey.

DO NOT CLOSE YOUR BROWSER WHILST COMPLETING THE SURVEY — YOU MUST COMPLETE THE SURVEY IN ONE SITTING.

Only the students of participating schools can complete the survey.

Each section presents instructions at the top on how to complete the questions in that section.

Some fields will offer suggestions as you type. Choose a suitable suggestion if one is presented, otherwise continue to type and your text will be accepted.

If you need to insert extra rows, press + to get another row, up to the maximum of 6.

You can navigate between sections using the Previous or Next buttons on each page.
