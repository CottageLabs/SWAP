Statement on information sharing

As a programme that supports students in their development and evolution in learning, SWAP monitors SWAP students with a view to improve the experience of students. SWAP also contributes to research into widening access across Scotland. For these reasons SWAP tracks students throughout their studies at both college and, subsequently, university. We do this by using the information students provide, for example on this form.

Any statistics and information SWAP subsequently publishes will never identify you as an individual.

The use of this data is listed below

  • we ask Colleges and Universities to give us course, entry, progression, attainment and retention information about SWAP students.
  • we may use the details on the registration form and use the information collected from Colleges and Universities for research and monitoring purposes and to publish statistics. These will be given only to Colleges, Universities, funding bodies and other educational establishments. No information that could identify you as an individual will be published.
  • we may enter into dialogue and share the details of your SWAP registration form and UCAS form with the universities and colleges to which you apply at any time during the application cycle. We do this only to clarify issues regarding your SWAP status or to resolve any admission issues relating to your offers from Universities.

By agreeing to submit our online registration form you give permission for us to use your information in this way.

If you do not want us to share your information you will not be able to use our online form and you must contact us (0131 650 6861) as you are still required to register as a SWAP student.

SWAP is a registered Data Controller and is fully compliant with the Principles and the other conditions set down in the Data Protection Act (DPA).
